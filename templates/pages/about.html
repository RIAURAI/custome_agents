"The average knowledge worker switches between 10+ apps every single day. That context-switching costs hours every week."

We built WorkHub because we lived that problem. Outlook for email. Teams for meetings. Slack for chat. A browser for everything else. It\'s exhausting.

Our Story

WorkHub is the answer to fragmented workdays. Instead of maintaining five different sessions across five different tools, we bring everything into one clean interface — and layer AI on top to handle the cognitive load.

The result: you spend less time triaging and more time actually thinking. Emails get summarized. Replies get drafted. Meetings show up without opening Teams. Slack channels are browsable without the endless scroll.

It\'s not magic. It\'s just good engineering applied to a real problem that every knowledge worker faces.

What We Built

Four tools that
work as one.

Email Hub — Outlook without Outlook

Connected directly to Microsoft Graph API. Your inbox loads inside WorkHub with unread counts, full message threads, and a clean reading experience. No separate browser tab needed.

AI Assistant — GPT-4o-mini for your inbox

A custom business-communication prompt drives every AI interaction. Summaries are concise, relevant, and action-focused. Draft replies sound natural — not like a bot wrote them.

Meeting Manager — Calendar without Teams

Uses Microsoft Calendar View API to fetch today's meetings in real time. Displays subject, start/end times, and one-click join links. Your schedule, where you actually work.

Slack Hub — Channels without Slack open

Full OAuth integration to your Slack workspace. Browse channels, read messages, send replies, and configure AI-driven auto-reply rules — all from your WorkHub dashboard.

Our Principles

How we
build things.

Security first, always

All tokens are Fernet-encrypted before database storage. OAuth flows follow PKCE best practices. We never log email content or message bodies.

Four features done right beats twelve done poorly

We could build more integrations. We chose to do fewer things exceptionally well. Every feature is pressure-tested before it ships.

Minimal data footprint

Email content is never persisted — fetched, rendered, discarded. Only what's needed to make the product work is stored. Your data stays yours.
