02

Click "Connect Microsoft" from your integrations page. You\'re redirected to Microsoft\'s secure OAuth login — sign in with your work or personal account and approve the permissions.

FAQ

Common questions,
straight answers.

Does WorkHub store my emails?
No. Email content is fetched from Microsoft Graph on demand and displayed. It is never written to our database.
Can I disconnect an integration anytime?
Yes. From the integrations page you can revoke access to Microsoft or Slack instantly. Tokens are deleted from the database immediately.
Which Microsoft account types are supported?
Both personal Microsoft accounts and work/school Microsoft 365 accounts work fine. If you can log in at login.microsoftonline.com, it will work.
Does the AI send replies automatically?
No — the AI drafts a reply for you to review. Nothing is sent without you explicitly sending it. Slack auto-reply rules are the exception, and those are configured manually by you.
Is it really free to use?
WorkHub is currently free. Individual use will always have a free tier. Team and enterprise plans are planned for the future.
