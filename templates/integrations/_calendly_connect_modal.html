Connect Calendly

Enter your Calendly OAuth app credentials — each company connects its own Calendly app

  1. Go to developer.calendly.com → Sign in with your Calendly account.
  2. Navigate to My Apps → Click Create New App.
  3. Fill in: App Name, Company URL, choose OAuth 2.0 as auth type.
  4. Set the Redirect URI to:
    {{ calendly_redirect_uri }}
  5. After creation, copy the Client ID and Client Secret from the app settings.
  6. Paste them below and click Save & Connect. You'll be redirected to Calendly to authorize.
