Connect WhatsApp Business

Set up your AI bot with WhatsApp Business API

1. Go to developers.facebook.com/apps
2. Click Create App → Select "Business" type → Fill in details
3. In your App dashboard → Add Product → Choose WhatsApp
4. Follow the setup wizard and link your WhatsApp Business Account (WABA)

1. In your app → WhatsApp → Getting Started
2. Copy your Phone Number ID (e.g. 123456789012345)
3. Copy your WhatsApp Business Account ID (WABA ID)
4. For production, go to Business Settings → System Users → Generate a permanent token with whatsapp_business_messaging permission

In your App → WhatsApp → Configuration → Webhooks:
• Callback URL: https://your-domain.com/social/webhooks/whatsapp/
• Verify Token: any secret string you choose (enter it below)
• Subscribe to: messages
